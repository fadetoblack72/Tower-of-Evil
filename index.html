Tower of Evil is a great silly little adventure where you get to have fun making choices... and dice rolling.

If you like playing through a game multiple times, making different choices each time, then Tower of Evil might be for you. Don't worry, it's short!

Simple

Tower of Evil is so easy to play that literacy is its toughest requirement.

Reading! Typing!

The whole game is text, so you just get to read what happens and type your choices in when prompted, then press enter!

Drama!

Sometimes you can even just press enter because the game is only making a dramatic pause! Such tension!!
